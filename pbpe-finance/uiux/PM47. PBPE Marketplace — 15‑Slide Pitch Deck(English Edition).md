# **PBPE Marketplace — 1‑Page Summary**

## **What is PBPE Marketplace?**

The PBPE Marketplace is the financial layer of Planetary Health OS.  
It transforms real-world climate, agriculture, and health impact into investable financial products.

---

## **How It Works**

1. **ImpactIntent** captures CO₂, soil, water, microbiome, and health improvements.
2. **PBPE Impact Engine** converts impact → PBPE value.
3. **PBPE Registry & Ledger** issues unique PBPE units with full traceability.
4. **PBPE Financial Products** (bonds, credits, insurance) are created.
5. **Investors** allocate capital.
6. **Settlement** occurs when impact is verified.
7. **40/40/20 Distribution** rewards actors and funds long-term resilience.

---

## **Why It Matters**

- Solves double-counting in carbon markets
- Monetizes soil regeneration and health improvement
- Creates a unified asset class across climate, agriculture, and health
- Enables automated MRV and real-time settlement
- Provides transparent, auditable impact finance

- **PBPE Impact Engine**

- **Marketplace API**
