# 📘 **docs/credits/PBPE_Credits_Specification.md（English Version）**


# PBPE Credits Specification  
**Version 1.0 — PBPE Marketplace / PBPE OS Layer 4**

PBPE Credits represent the standardized impact units issued within the PBPE Marketplace.  
They quantify climate, biosecurity, food system, and nutritional improvements generated by  
MBT55, AGRIX, and the MBT‑Biosecurity‑Engine.

PBPE Credits consist of four primary categories:

- **Carbon PBPE**
- **Biosecurity PBPE**
- **Food Loss PBPE**
- **Quality PBPE**

Each credit type is backed by verifiable impact data and follows a unified issuance framework.

---

# 1. PBPE Credits Overview

| Credit Type       | Impact Domain                         | Unit Basis                          | Primary Data Source |
|-------------------|----------------------------------------|--------------------------------------|----------------------|
| Carbon PBPE       | Greenhouse gas reduction               | 1 PBPE = 1 tCO₂e                     | MBT55 / AGRIX / MRV  |
| Biosecurity PBPE  | Microbial & pathogen risk reduction    | Biosecurity Index × Area × Duration  | MBT‑Biosecurity‑Engine |
| Food Loss PBPE    | Food loss prevention                   | Food Loss Avoided × β                | Retail / Logistics / MBT55 |
| Quality PBPE      | Nutritional & quality enhancement      | Nutrition Index Improvement × Volume | AGRIX / MBT Nutrition Index |

---

# 2. Common Specification

## 2.1 Metadata Structure

```json
{
  "credit_id": "string",
  "type": "carbon | biosecurity | food_loss | quality",
  "project_id": "string",
  "vintage_year": 2026,
  "amount_pbpe": 1000000,
  "methodology_id": "PBPE-METH-001",
  "mrv_reference": "MBT-Biosecurity-Engine v1.2"
}
````

## 2.2 Issuance Workflow

1. **Impact Measurement**  
    Data collected from MBT‑Biosecurity‑Engine, AGRIX, field sensors, and supply chain systems.
    
2. **Impact Verification (MRV)**
    
    - Automated MRV via ImpactWebhook
    - Optional third‑party verification
3. **PBPE Calculation**  
    Based on the formulas defined for each credit type.
    
4. **Issuance**
    
    - Recorded in the PBPE Ledger
    - Listed on PBPE Marketplace

---

# 3. Carbon PBPE

## 3.1 Impact Scope

- Waste methane avoidance
- Food loss reduction
- Soil carbon sequestration
- Chemical fertilizer reduction
- Livestock methane reduction
- Rice methane reduction
- Biomass increase

## 3.2 Calculation Formula

[ \text{Carbon PBPE} = \text{Verified GHG Reduction (tCO₂e)} ]

## 3.3 Data Sources

- MBT55 fermentation data
- AGRIX productivity data
- Soil carbon models
- Food loss models

---

# 4. Biosecurity PBPE

## 4.1 Impact Scope

- Microbial risk reduction
- Pathogen suppression
- Ecosystem stability improvement

## 4.2 Calculation Formula

[ \text{Biosecurity PBPE} = \text{Risk Reduction Index} \times \text{Coverage Area (ha)} \times \text{Duration (years)} ]

## 4.3 Data Sources

- MBT‑Biosecurity‑Engine (Hypercycle Stability, Biosecurity Risk Index)

---

# 5. Food Loss PBPE

## 5.1 Impact Scope

- Retail & logistics food loss reduction
- MBT55‑driven freshness extension

## 5.2 Calculation Formula

[ \text{Food Loss PBPE} = \text{Food Loss Avoided (t)} \times \beta ]

β = tCO₂e conversion factor + food security coefficient

---

# 6. Quality PBPE

## 6.1 Impact Scope

- Nutritional enhancement (lycopene, polyphenols, minerals)
- Antioxidant capacity improvement
- Freshness stability improvement

## 6.2 Calculation Formula

[ \text{Quality PBPE} = \text{Nutrition Index Improvement} \times \text{Volume (t)} ]

## 6.3 Data Sources

- MBT Nutrition Index (AGRIX × MBT55)

---
