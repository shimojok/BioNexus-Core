# **Chapter X — PBPE Marketplace: A Financial Layer for Planetary Value**

## **X.1 Overview**

The PBPE Marketplace is the financial layer of Planetary Health OS.  
While the operating system computes and verifies planetary impact across agriculture, climate, and health,  
the PBPE Marketplace transforms this verified impact into investable financial products.

It enables PBPE (Planetary Bio‑Positive Effect) to function as a new asset class—  
backed by real, measured, and audited planetary outcomes.

## **X.2 From Impact to Financial Products**

The marketplace is built on a simple but powerful pipeline:

**Intent → PBPE Value → Financial Product → Capital Flow → New Impact**

1. **Intent Layer** computes PBPE value from real‑world data.
2. **PBPE Registry & Ledger** issues unique PBPE units and prevents double counting.
3. **PBPE Financial Products** package PBPE value into bonds, credits, insurance, and protection products.
4. **Investors** allocate capital into these products.
5. **Connect Layer** distributes value (40/40/20) and reinvests into further planetary regeneration.

This creates a regenerative loop where **impact generates value, and value funds more impact**.

## **X.3 PBPE Financial Product Families**

The PBPE Marketplace supports a standardized family of financial products:

- PBPE Carbon Income Bonds
- PBPE Regenerative Agriculture Bonds
- PBPE Health Impact Bonds
- Soil Carbon Credits
- Coffee Resilience Credits
- Microbiome Health Credits
- Preventive Health Insurance
- Agricultural Protection Products

All products follow the PBPE‑FIN‑001 schema, ensuring global comparability and auditability.

## **X.4 PBPE Registry & Ledger**

The PBPE Registry & Ledger provides:

- Unique PBPE issuance
- Full traceability
- Anti‑double‑counting
- Settlement records
- Distribution records
- Audit trails

This solves the transparency and integrity issues that plague traditional carbon markets.

## **X.5 PBPE Risk & Rating Engine**

To support institutional investment, the marketplace includes a scientific rating engine:

- Intent Reliability Score (IRS)
- Data Quality Score (DQS)
- Regional Risk Score (RRS)
- PBPE Volatility Index (PVI)
- PBPE Default Probability (PDP)

These combine into a **Composite Risk Score (PCRS)**,  
mapped to **PBPE‑A / PBPE‑B / PBPE‑C / PBPE‑D**.

## **X.6 Settlement and Value Distribution**

When PBPE value is realized:

- PBPE is computed and verified
- Settlement is executed
- Value is distributed via the 40/40/20 model
- Ledger entries are recorded
- Investor dashboards update in real time

This ensures that **those who generate planetary value are rewarded**,  
while long‑term funds for climate and health resilience grow.

## **X.7 Investor and Issuer Experience**

The marketplace provides:

### **For Investors**

- PBPE portfolios
- Yield, risk, and impact analytics
- ESG‑aligned investment opportunities

### **For Issuers**

- Product issuance
- MRV monitoring
- Settlement management
- Impact reporting

## **X.8 A New Class of Planetary Assets**

PBPE Marketplace introduces a new asset class:

- Rooted in real‑world impact
- Verified through automated MRV
- Recorded in a transparent ledger
- Rated through a scientific risk engine
- Distributed through a regenerative value model

This is not ESG as an overlay.  
It is **finance built on planetary health as the primary source of value**.
